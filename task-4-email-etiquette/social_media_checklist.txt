Social Media Guidelines (Do’s and Don’ts)

Do’s:
- Keep your profile clean and professional
- Share informative and meaningful posts
- Be respectful while interacting with others
- Use strong privacy settings
- Think carefully before posting anything online
- Build a positive digital presence

Don’ts:
- Avoid sharing confidential or personal data
- Do not post negative or harmful content
- Avoid spreading unverified information
- Do not engage in online arguments or hate speech
- Never click on unknown or suspicious links
- Avoid oversharing on public platforms
